**X-TRANS Ltd** was established in Piraeus in order to operate in the area of transport services, customs clearance, storage, delivery and security of goods. Our long experience and the increasing needs of the market prompted us to create and develop new tools for better and more direct service to our customers.

For this purpose we created modern facilities in Piraeus — at the Schistos Industrial Park, Street 3, No. 1111, Block 11, P.O. Box 3118 (postal code 18863) — that provide easy and convenient access, helping our customers achieve faster and more economical solutions in the receipt and delivery of their goods. We provide a wide range of professional services in air freight, sea freight and road transport, in forwarding, door-to-door, personal items, customs clearance, and the insurance and storage of goods on import and export.

We operate proprietary transport modes that cover all transportation needs from and to any destination within Greece.
